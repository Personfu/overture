#Arizona should not normalize private #ALPR grids. We are the Wild West, not a private location-tracking test range. 🏜️

England is closing Traffic England on 6/30/2026 and moving public road information toward navigation apps, National Highways pages, and professional data channels. That is the warning: public-road intelligence must not disappear into private systems.

## Hard-hitting version

Arizona should not normalize private ALPR grids.

Flock-style systems are not ordinary cameras. They can create searchable vehicle-location histories. If public agencies or private communities deploy them, residents deserve the contract, camera map, retention rules, audit logs, sharing partners, warrant policy, and renewal vote.

England closing Traffic England is the warning sign: public-road intelligence can move away from public portals and toward private channels.

Arizona should move the other way: more public accountability, not less.
